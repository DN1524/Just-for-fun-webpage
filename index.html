It is the 31st century, and mankind is once again at war.

The battlefields of the future are dominated by huge robotic war machines known as battlemechs.

Piloting these awesome weapons of war are men and women, the elite of the elite, knowing that each battle could be their last.

They are... Mechwarriors.

My Thoughts on Mechwarrior

I really do not know much about the story of the franchise... I just know that the game was pretty fun and played MW3 to MW4 mercs. When MWO came out, I gave it a try but did not really like too much. It reminded me too much about World of Tanks and also I did not want to pay $100 + on mechs I loved to play in the previous MW's...

My Favorite Mechs

Annihilator - Assault Mech

This was favorite Assault mech in MW3. It is a very slow mech, but can be heavily armed and deal major damage especially when armed with multiple ultra AC 20 cannons.

Thor - Heavy Mech

This was my first favorite mech. I When I first played this mech in MW3, I really liked how it looked and I loved to use PPC's and LBX cannons. When I started playing MW4, I changed to ER Heavy Lasers with LBX 10's. What I really liked about the Thor in MW4 was it's good maneuverability and it's 360 degree torso twist.

Cougar - Medium Mech

I started playing the cougar in MW4. I really like this mech because it is fast and a little harder to hit since it is short. I armed this mech like I would my Thor, with a couple of Large lasers and an LBX 10 cannon.

Osiris - Light Mech

I don't normally play light mechs, but when I do, I prefer the Osiris.

Anyway... I liked the Osiris better than the other light mechs in MW4 because I liked the way it looked was able to carry weapons bigger than machine guns.
